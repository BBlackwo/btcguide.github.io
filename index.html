--- title: 10x Security Guide ---

How to store bitcoin without a single point of failure. By @mflaxman.

Purpose

Multisig security is a difference in kind and not in degree. The ability to not suffer loss while making 1 (or more) catastrophic failures in securing your bitcoin is unrivaled by any other security offering. In order to do this, your multisig scheme must not contain any single point of failure. By using a system that is fundamentally much more secure, you can move much faster (with less caution) through each step and still attain far higher levels of security vs any single-key system. This guide will show you how.

The ultimate purpose of this guide is to make multisig accessible to a tech-savvy audience who does not write code. We will not require use of the command-line. Unfortunately, right now there are some bugs/vulnerabilities that do require some command-line experience. For users who are not comfortable using the command-line, you are encouraged to wait for a future release. You can track progress on known bugs/issues here.
